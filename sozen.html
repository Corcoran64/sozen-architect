Architecture and Interiors for Houses

SoZen Architects is an emerging architecture and interior design studio committed to delivering supremely liveable homes.

We’ve come to know our clients well; you live the busy life and crave a calm space to retreat to at the end of the day.

A modern home where everything has its place. A home that is perhaps a little bold and adventurous, but remarkably functional and efficient too. You love your neighbourhood and its corner coffee shop, and want a home that belongs. A house that is a little brave, a little different, but intrinsically you.
